Frequently Asked Questions

Are Google Ads better than Meta Ads?

Google Ads target search intent, meaning the leads are generally warmer and ready to buy immediately. Meta Ads are better for visual products and cheaper lead volume.

Why is my CPC so high?

High Cost Per Click is usually due to a low Quality Score, which happens when your landing page experience or ad copy isn't perfectly aligned with the search keyword.
