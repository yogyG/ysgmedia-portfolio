Frequently Asked Questions

How much do I need to spend on Meta Ads?

It depends on your industry and goals. Local businesses can start with ₹500/day, while national campaigns require higher budgets for optimal testing.

Do I need to supply the video creatives?

We highly recommend you provide raw footage of your business. However, our creative team handles all the editing, scripting, and graphic design to make the ad convert.

How quickly will I see leads?

Once the campaigns are launched, leads usually start coming in within 24 to 48 hours. However, full algorithmic optimization takes about 7 days.
